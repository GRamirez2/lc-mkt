Introducing LivingConnected
Hello Team!
We are happy to announce a new health benefit for all eligible employees and family members.

If you or your dependent(s) are enrolled in the company health insurance plan and have been diagnosed with diabetes, you qualify for a free new benefit called the LivingConnected Diabetes Program from CCS Medical.

All LivingConnected participants will receive the following supplies and services at $0 copay!
  • State-of-the-Art Cellular Glucose Meter
  • Diabetes Supplies: Test Strips, Lancing Device, Lancets, etc.
  • Assistance and Support:
    1. Free home delivery of supplies as you need them, with no out-of-pocket expense
    2. Alerts and coaching from a Registered Nurse when your readings are out of range
    3. Live, ongoing personalized health coaching from Certified Diabetes Educators
    4. Online portal and smartphone application where you can access and share your results
    5. 24/7/365 toll-free access to live nursing support
CCS Medical is committed to helping you manage your diabetes on your terms, in a way that fits your lifestyle.
To learn more and sign up, go to livingconnected.me

If you would like more information, please contact us directly at 1.800.966.2046.

Keep a look out for additional information from LivingConnected about the advantages of the new program.

We are delighted to offer this new benefit and encourage you to take advantage of it. If you have any questions about the program, don’t hesitate to reach out.

Have a great day!
