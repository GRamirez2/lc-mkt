  • Getting Started

    • Sign Up & Choose Your Meter

      LivingConnected offers a growing number of smart connected devices, including cellular and Bluetooth glucose meters.

    • Receive Your Free Welcome Kit

      Once you sign up and select your free new smart meter, you will receive a Welcome Kit containing everything you need to get started and on your way to better health!

    • Schedule Your Welcome Call

      After you receive your Welcome Kit, you will get a welcome call from a trained clinician. Get your questions answered, meter training, and health coaching.

  • Program Perks

    • Live Monitoring & Coaching

      Real-time data from your smart meter allows our Registered Nurses to observe glucose readings on a 24/7 basis. They can reach out to you by phone to ensure that you are okay and know what to do when your readings are out of range.

    • Ongoing, Personalized Support

      If you need extra help managing your glucose levels, you are also eligible for ongoing, personalized diabetes coaching conducted by CCS Medical's Certified Diabetes Educators, Registered Pharmacists, Dietitians and Nurses.

    • Enjoy all the extras!

      Receive unlimited refills of diabetes supplies including glucose strips, lancing devices, lancets and control solution. Access a personal dashboard of your glucose data through an online portal and share the data with any member of your family or health care team.
